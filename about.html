OUR STORY

Heer's Collection is more than just a brand — it is a family dream created with dedication, trust and passion. The company is proudly owned by Mr. Malik Nasir. Under the leadership of Managing Directors Mr. Malik Hasan and Mr. Malik Umair Nasir, the vision of our business continues to grow. The name "Heer's Collection" was inspired by Mrs. Hira Hasan Malik, the beloved wife of Mr. Malik Hasan. Today she proudly serves as CEO and continues contributing with vision, support and inspiration. Our mission is simple: To provide premium quality, excellent service, and build long-term trust. We are proud to be a family business and we are here to provide the best service and good quality. Thank you for being part of our journey.
