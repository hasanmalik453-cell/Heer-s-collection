OUR STORY

Heer's Collection is more than fashion — it is a family dream built with dedication, trust and love. The foundation of our business was laid by Mr. Malik Nasir, whose vision, values and commitment created the path for growth. Today the company is proudly managed by Mr. Malik Hasan Nasir and Mr. Malik Umair Nasir who continue the mission of delivering premium quality and excellent customer service. The identity of Heer's Collection was inspired by Mrs. Hira Hasan Malik, whose name became the inspiration behind our brand. As CEO, she represents elegance, family values and customer trust. We believe fashion should combine quality, comfort and confidence. Every collection is selected carefully because our mission is simple: Provide the best quality, serve every customer with respect, and create a trusted family brand.
